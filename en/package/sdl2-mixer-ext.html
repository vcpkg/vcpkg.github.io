sdl2-mixer-ext

Version: 2.6.0
Port-Version: 1
Description: An audio mixer library based on the SDL library, a fork of SDL_mixer
License: Zlib OR LGPL-2.1-or-later OR GPL-2.0-or-later OR GPL-3.0-or-later
Last Updated: 2024-02-23
Homepage: https://wohlsoft.github.io/SDL-Mixer-X
Documentation: N/A
Supported Platforms/Architectures: arm-uwp, arm64-windows, x64-linux, x64-osx, x64-uwp, x64-windows, x64-windows-static, x86-windows


Dependencies

  • sdl2
  • vcpkg-cmake (host: true)
  • vcpkg-cmake-config (host: true)


Installation Instructions

Classic Mode:

 vcpkg install sdl2-mixer-ext

Manifest Mode:

 vcpkg add port sdl2-mixer-ext


Available Versions

2.6.0#1 2.6.0#0 2023-05-04#1 2023-05-04#0


Features

>cmd - Use external command for playing music (Linux only).
No Dependencies
>ffmpeg - Use FFMPEG to play WMA and AAC audio formats.
Dependencies:
  • ffmpeg (default-features: false)
>fluidsynth - Use FluidSynth to play MIDI audio format.
Dependencies:
  • fluidsynth (default-features: false)
>libadlmidi - Use libADLMIDI to play XMI, MUS, IMF and regular MIDI audio format with OPL3 (YMF262) emulator.
Dependencies:
  • libadlmidi (default-features: false)
>libflac - Use libflac to play FLAC audio format.
Dependencies:
  • libflac
>libgme - Use libgme to play chip music (AY, GBS, GYM, HES, KSS, NSF/NSFE, SAP, SPC, VGM/VGZ).
Dependencies:
  • libgme (default-features: false)
>libmodplug - Use libmodplug to play tracker music including exclusive formats (AMS, DMF, DSM, MT2).
Dependencies:
  • libmodplug
>libopnmidi - Use libOPNMIDI to play MIDI and RMI audio format with OPN2 (YM2612) emulator.
Dependencies:
  • libopnmidi (default-features: false)
>libvorbis - Use libvorbis to play OGG audio format.
Dependencies:
  • libvorbis
>libxmp - Use libxmp to play tracker music including exclusive formats (ABK, DIGI, DTM, EMOD, FLX, FNK, GDM, IMF, J2B, LIQ, M15, MFP, MGT, MMDC, MTN, RTM, SFX, SPM, STIM, STX, WOW).
Dependencies:
  • libxmp (default-features: false)
>mpg123 - Use mpg123 to play MP3 audio format.
Dependencies:
  • mpg123
>nativemidi - Use Native MIDI Player to play MIDI audio format.
No Dependencies
>opusfile - Use opusfile to play Opus audio format.
Dependencies:
  • opusfile
>pxtone - Use pxtone to play PPTUNE and PTCOP audio formats.
No Dependencies
>timidity - Use Timidity to play MIDI audio format.
No Dependencies
>wavpack - Use wavpack to play VW audio format.
Dependencies:
  • wavpack
